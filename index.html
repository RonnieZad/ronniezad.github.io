NOT ALONE UG

who we
are

Not Alone UG is a project involving students in high school empowering other people in other communities to look at the brighter side of life. As the external coordinator and managing director of this small organisation, working with friends with great dyanmism we identified a family. Then provided them with smattering groceries and got to know them at personal level.

The mission of NOT ALONE UG is to contribute to encourage and remind people that there is more life, and also look up to the bette side not the past.

Ronald Reagan once said, "We can't help everyone but everyone can help someone." Though the first visit we espied harsh lving conditions of the family. And we are ardently trying to ameliorate their conditions.
I am humbly requesting for your suppoer to help this family and mant others see a brighter side of life

What
we do

NOT ALONE UG is committed to supporting innovative, non-religious and non-politically affiliated initiatives working towards gender equality, child protection and education.

The final objective of NOT ALONE UG is to set the basis for a grant-making activity that enjoys a growing geographical presence, whilst ensuring long-term support to the majority of ongoing projects in order to contribute to their long-lasting impact.

The selection process ensures the implementation of thorough due-diligence checks and monitoring and evaluation practices whilst setting the base for the creation of meaningful and impactful partnerships.
